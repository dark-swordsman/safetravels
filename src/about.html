What's the deal?

When you're planning on going to a new place, you want to have your wits about you. SafeTravels isn't about scaring you into picking pricey accomodation, it's about the need for more serious information about where you end up.

We felt that it was important to use powerful tools like TripAdvisor and EventBrite in order to make sure users get what they want, where they want--and safely. From families to frequent travelers, hopefully SafeTravels will lead you down the right road.
